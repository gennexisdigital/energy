FAQ

Questions, answered.

What exactly is an AI Ambassador?

An interactive AI brand representative — a digital version of your best concierge, salesperson, or promoter. It greets customers, answers questions, recommends products, and guides people, on screens and kiosks in your physical spaces, 24/7.

How is this different from a chatbot?

A chatbot is a text box. An AI Ambassador is a presence: a face, a voice, and a personality built on your brand — trained on your products and service standards, not the open internet, and designed for face-to-face customer engagement.

Which languages does it support?

40+ languages, handled automatically. The Ambassador detects and responds in the customer's language — no hand-offs, no waiting for a translator.

Will it replace my staff?

No — and that's a principle, not a footnote. We believe AI should support people, not replace them. Oomph! AI covers the hours and languages your team can't, and hands the moments that matter to your people.

How long does deployment take?

Typically weeks, not months: we capture your expertise, design the Ambassador, and deploy to your locations — one or hundreds — with the same quality everywhere.

What insight do I get from it?

The dashboard shows exactly what customers ask, which languages dominate your floor, and which products or services generate the most interest — insight your human team alone could never capture at scale.
